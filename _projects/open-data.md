A curated list of open data resources for metro Atlanta and the state of Georgia. This community-maintained directory helps researchers, journalists, developers, and civic hackers find the public datasets they need.

## What's Included

- City of Atlanta datasets
- Georgia state data portals
- Transportation and transit data
- Census and demographic resources
- Environmental and geographic data

## Get Involved

Know of a dataset we're missing? Contributions welcome via pull request.
