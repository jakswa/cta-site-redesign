Interactive map of green career opportunities in Atlanta, designed specifically for youth in the Peoplestown neighborhood. A companion project to the ATL Green Buildings Map, helping young people discover pathways into environmental and sustainability careers.

## Goals

- Connect Peoplestown youth with green job opportunities
- Visualize environmental career pathways in the Atlanta area
- Support workforce development in sustainability sectors

## Get Involved

This project welcomes contributors interested in:

- JavaScript and mapping libraries
- Environmental justice and workforce development
- Youth program design and outreach
