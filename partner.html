Our Process

We've developed a lightweight process to ensure projects are a good fit and set up for success:

  1. Submit a Proposal
    Tell us about your organization and the challenge you're facing. Don't worry if you don't have all the answers - we can help you figure things out.
  2. Initial Review
    We review proposals for alignment with our mission and capacity. We'll reach out with questions or to schedule a conversation.
  3. Discovery
    We'll dig deeper into stakeholder needs, accessibility requirements, and sustainability planning to make sure we build the right thing.
  4. Project Planning
    Together we'll develop an initial high-level project plan with clear goals, milestones, and success metrics.
  5. Build Together
    We present the project to our community at hack nights to recruit volunteers. You'll work alongside us as a partner, not just a client.

Note: As part of our process, we ask project leads to complete worksheets on Impact Measurement, Racial Equity & Accessibility, and Resourcing & Feasibility. If you don't have all the answers, that's ok! We can help you work through them.
