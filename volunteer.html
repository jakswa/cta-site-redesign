Get Involved

No experience necessary - just bring curiosity and a willingness to help

First-Timer Guide

New to Civic Tech Atlanta? Here's everything you need to know to feel confident at your first hack night.

What to Expect

Hack nights happen on the 2nd and 4th Mondays of each month. A typical evening looks like:

  • Arrive - Doors open, grab food, mingle
  • Kickoff - Announcements and project updates
  • Hack time - Break into project groups and work
  • Wrap up - Share progress and wins

Check Meetup for exact times. Don't worry about coming "prepared" - just show up! We welcome all skill levels and backgrounds.

How to Pick a Project

After announcements, project leads give quick pitches about what they're working on. You can:

  • Join an existing project that sounds interesting
  • Float between projects to learn about each one
  • Help with general tasks (documentation, research, outreach)
  • Propose your own project idea

Check out our Projects page ahead of time to see what's active.

Who to Talk To

Look for these folks - they're happy to help you get oriented:

Organizers

Run the hack night, make announcements, and can point you in the right direction. Find them up front!

Project Leads

Know their project inside and out. Ask them about goals, what help they need, and how to contribute.

Fellow Newcomers

You're not alone! Look for other first-timers and learn together. We all started somewhere.

Join Our Community

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know how to code?

Nope! We need all kinds of skills: writing, design, research, project management, community outreach, subject matter expertise, and more. Some of our most valuable contributors don't write a line of code.

I'm a student/between jobs/new to tech. Can I still help?

Absolutely. Civic tech is a great way to build skills, contribute to real projects, and connect with others in the community. Everyone starts somewhere, and we're happy to help you grow.

What if I can't commit to every hack night?

That's totally fine! Come when you can. Some folks come every week, others pop in monthly. There are also ways to contribute asynchronously via Slack and GitHub.

Can I bring my own project idea?

Yes! We love new ideas. Check out our project proposal process to learn how to pitch your idea to the group.

Is there food?

Usually yes! We typically have pizza and snacks available. Let us know about dietary restrictions.
