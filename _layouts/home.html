Our Principles

These values guide everything we do.

Solve real problems

We solve problems that real people have, we make sure what we build works for them, and we continuously improve it.

Work with, not for, the people we serve

We start with our users, the people affected by the service, and understand and respect their needs throughout the process.

Work lean, iterate quickly

We get a working minimum viable product in front of users as early as possible and make continuous improvements based on how they use the service.

Non-partisan, but not neutral

We are an alliance of non-partisan groups, but that non-partisanship does not imply neutrality.

Default to open

We work in the open and collaborate with the community to help make programs and services better for everyone.
